Altcoin Liquidity Cycles Explained

Liquidity, not headlines, drives rotations. Alt seasons begin when depth and borrowed liquidity rise faster than fully-diluted valuations. Watching order-book depth and stablecoin issuance beats chasing social buzz.

Three phases.

  • Accumulation: Depth improves, FDV stays flat, funding neutral. Best to accumulate high-circulation names and avoid ultra-high FDV pre-launch tokens.
  • Markup: Funding turns positive, basis widens, narratives go mainstream. Trim leverage; shift to spot and trail stops.
  • Distribution: Depth thins while FDV balloons; insiders unlock; funding spikes. Move to cash or majors, avoid catching knives.
