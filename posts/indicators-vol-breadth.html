Indicators

Volatility + Breadth Combo

Why combine them? Volatility shows stress; breadth shows participation. Together they flag when risk-on shifts are durable versus fake-outs.

Inputs.

  • Realized vs. implied volatility spread (e.g., BTC RV vs. DVOL, SPX HV vs. VIX).
  • Advance/decline breadth or % of assets above 50D MA in your universe (majors, alts, FX pairs).
  • Volume/OBV confirmation to filter illiquid pops.

Signal construction.

  • Risk-on trigger: IV > RV by <= 5 vol points and breadth rising above 55% of assets over 50D MA.
  • Risk-off trigger: IV > RV by > 10 vol points and breadth drops below 40%.
  • Neutral: anything in between; avoid overtrading.

Use cases.

  • Adjust position leverage: full size only in risk-on regime; half size in neutral; defensive hedges in risk-off.
  • Time entries: wait for first higher low after a fresh risk-on trigger to reduce whipsaw.
  • Portfolio tilt: overweight momentum baskets when breadth confirms; pivot to mean-reversion when it fades.
