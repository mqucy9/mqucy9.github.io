Carry vs. Volatility: When to Stand Aside

Ad slot — insert tag

Context. FX carry trades pay you to hold higher-yielding currencies, but returns collapse when volatility spikes. A simple rule-set keeps you out of trouble.

Inputs to watch.

  • 3m implied volatility vs. its 6m median for each leg in the basket.
  • Cross-asset vol: VIX and MOVE; if both are elevated, correlations rise and carry suffers.
  • Rate path surprises: track OIS-implied changes after central-bank meetings.

Rules.

  • Run carry only when implied vol is below 1.1x its 6m median and falling for five sessions.
  • Exit or halve size when vol breaks above that threshold or after a hawkish surprise.
  • Cap basket risk: no more than 2% of account value per basket; max 0.5% per pair.
  • Hedge tail risk with cheap out-of-the-money options when vol is low; they pay off when cycles turn.
