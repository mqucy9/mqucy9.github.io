R-Multiple Breakout System

Goal. A simple, reusable breakout template that scales across gold, BTC, and EURUSD while keeping risk constant.

Setup. Use a 20-day Donchian channel for entries and a 10-day channel for exits. Define 1R as 1% of account equity. Position size = (1R / ATR(14)). For instruments with higher volatility (BTC), cap at 0.5R per trade.

Rules.

  • Initial stop at 1x ATR below/above entry; trail with 10-day low/high after breakeven.
  • Take partial profits at +2R, move stop to entry; hold remainder until 10-day exit triggers.
  • Maximum 4 concurrent positions; never more than 2 correlated assets at once.

Why it works. Most edge comes from risk discipline, not prediction. The system limits downside, rides occasional fat-tail trends, and is transparent enough for readers and search engines alike.
