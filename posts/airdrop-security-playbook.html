Smart-contract safety.

  • Simulate the transaction (Tenderly/Blocknative) before signing; inspect for unexpected approvals.
  • Limit token approvals; prefer permit signatures with narrow scopes and expirations.
  • Revoke approvals weekly using a trusted revoker; log what you revoke.

Avoiding sybil flags.

  • Do not reuse identical on-chain patterns across multiple wallets (identical timing and bridges).
  • Keep interaction history organic: swaps, bridges, NFT mints across weeks鈥攏ot a single burst.
  • Avoid moving funds between your own wallets right before snapshot windows.

After claiming. Swap a small test amount first to ensure token transferability. Move claimed tokens to cold storage only after approvals are revoked. Keep gas for one extra transaction in case you must react quickly.
