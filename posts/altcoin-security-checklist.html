Security Checklist for New Tokens

Goal. Avoid high FDV traps and smart-contract blowups by running a short, repeatable checklist before taking any position.

Token economics.

  • Circulating vs. FDV: avoid FDV above 5x circulating unless depth is growing fast.
  • Unlock calendar: map monthly unlocks; cut size ahead of heavy cliffs.
  • Treasury transparency: multisig signers, timelocks, and on-chain reporting cadence.

Smart-contract risk.

  • Audit recency and scope; prefer audits that include governance and upgradability paths.
  • Admin keys and pause functions: who holds them, and what is the response plan?
  • Oracle design: if price feed relies on thin DEX liquidity, slippage attacks are likely.

Market structure.

  • Order-book depth: position size should not exceed 10% of visible 1% depth.
  • Venue diversity: healthy projects trade on multiple venues; single-venue risk is high.
  • Funding and basis: extreme positive funding without depth is a liquidation trap.
