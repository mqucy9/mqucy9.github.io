Liquidity Heatmap Indicator

Concept. A cross-asset heatmap shows when liquidity is expanding or contracting. Combine it with trend signals to avoid fake breakouts.

Inputs.

  • FX basis: EURUSD and USDJPY cross-currency basis spreads.
  • Stablecoin growth: 7d change in USDT+USDC supply.
  • Funding rates: BTC, ETH, and top 5 alt perpetuals.
  • Equity vol: VIX term structure (front vs. 3m).
  • Credit: CDX IG spread direction.
Ad slot — insert tag

Scoring.

  • +1 when each input improves (basis up, supply up, funding near zero, VIX curve in contango, credit tighter).
  • -1 when deteriorating. Sum the scores; +3 or higher = expansion, -3 or lower = contraction.

Usage. Only take breakout trades when the heatmap is >= +2. In contraction, prefer mean-reversion or stay in cash. Recompute daily; publish the heatmap chart to keep this page fresh for SEO.
